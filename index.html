Processing for Delta-8 THC & Delta-9 THC

The legal landscape for hemp-derived cannabinoids like Delta-8 THC and Delta-9 THC (with less than 0.3% THC by dry weight) is complex and varies by jurisdiction. Securing payment processing for these products requires specialized knowledge.

Blackbird Merchant Services works with financial institutions that understand these nuances. We can help you establish merchant accounts if your business model and products meet the current legal and banking requirements. Key considerations include:

  • Source of Hemp: Ensuring products are derived from legally cultivated hemp.
  • Lab Testing (COAs): Third-party Certificates of Analysis are crucial for product verification.
  • Marketing Compliance: Avoiding unsubstantiated health claims and ensuring age verification.
  • Shipping Restrictions: Adherence to state-by-state shipping laws for Delta-8/9 products.

We stay updated on the evolving regulations to provide the most current advice and solutions for your Delta-variant business.
